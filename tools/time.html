Javascript Time Tester

Not all time strings work. But here are a few that should. The actual implementation will depend on your browser.

Should work:

  • ISO-8601 yyyy-mm-ddThh:mm:ss.sssZ (prefered)
  • March 2 2015
  • March 2 2015 12:45
  • 2014/11/02 12:54
  • 12:53 2014/11/02
  • 2014
  • 2/3/2014 ( MM/DD/YYYY)
  • Mon, 25 Dec 1995 13:30:00 GMT
  • 2011-11-12

This does not include:

  • dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm:ss (it will be passed as mm/dd/yyyy hh:mm:ss american style)
  • yyyy-mm-dd hh:mm:ss
